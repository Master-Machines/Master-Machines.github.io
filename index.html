2D Collision.
3D World.

Breaking Collision is a puzzle game where you solve puzzles in a 3D world with the help of 2D mechanics. You play as an alien who wakes up in a huge cave. You explore this cave in order to find out who you are, where you are and why you are alone in this mystical place.

Technology

Breaking Collision uses the Unity game engine. The game will run on mid-range Windows computers.
Dice In Wine consists of 15 students from the University of Skövde in Sweden. Work began in March 2013, though a prototype of the game was made earlier in the year by project lead Karl Zylinski.

Design

Simon Götborg and Neophos Pojke are the designers on the team. They designed the puzzles and implemented them in Unity.

Graphics

Johan Zdybal (graphics lead) and Cheri Nossborn are 3D modellers. Sofie Johansson and Fredrik Emanuelsson are 2D artits. Kristina Åsén is our animator.

Programming

David Norberg (programming lead), Petter Henriksson, Derek Schindhelm, and Karl Zylinski are our programmers. Karl also helps out with design as he came up with the idea of the game.

Audio

Gustav Sverin (music lead), Joakim Karlberg, and Eric Lundh are responsible for all of the music heard in Breaking Collision. Jonatan Läkarn Sjöberg is the sound designer on the team.
